Our history.

Our business started in 2020 in the city of São Paulo Brazil, as a family production for friends and relatives, our products have stood out and pleased our customers to the point that our orders have increased exponentially in recent years, we always value quality products and we have perfected production techniques every day which makes our cakes fluffy and tasty with juicy fillings, but without too much sugar And we also make customized products for those who can't eat sugar and those who have allergies to certain ingredients, showing our concern for health and exceptional taste.

Our products

Our products are simple or filled cakes decorated or not, for recreational use or for parties, birthday, baptism, weddings we also work with Brazilian snacks such as coxinhas, risolis, cheese balls, buns stuffed with cheese, Calabrese sausage with tomato and onion, and chicken with cottage cheese, we make Brazilian sweets called brigadeiros, beijinhos, dulce de leche many of them customized in flower and gourmet shapes.
